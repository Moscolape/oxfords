ABOUT OXFORDS

About Oxfords

Oxfords International School is situated in the central part of Aba - Abia's largest, commercial and most populated city.

Oxfords International School was founded by Ineh Moses Giovanni, the Co-ordinator of Academic Planning, in August 2003 with the cardinal objective of providing high quality standard education to the Nigerian population at considerably low tuition fee.

The school has three campuses: Secondary, Primary, and Nursery and organizes special extension programmes for candidates sitting for SAT, GMAT, GRE, TOEFE, and TSF exams.

Oxfords International School accepts for admission, children who will benefit from a personalised education which seeks to develop an appreciation for one's heritage, to enjoy learning, to think critically, to communicate effectively, and to balance their intellectual, emotional and spiritual development.

Our Mission

Oxfords International School has a vital and distinctive mission to perform in addressing the general educational needs of the students. Among the operational objectives of Oxfords International School are;

1.) To provide an all-round education featuring a combination of theory and practice, primarily through appropriate classroom, laboratory and cooperative/clinical education experiences. Our challenging academic programmes presents every kind of opportunity for discerning one's ability and learning style.

2.) To provide services and educational experiences to assist students in determining and reaching their educational goals.

3.) To provide opportunities for students to develop the skills needed to enter and succeed in higher educational programmes in Nigeria and abroad.

4.) To guide the young into adulthood equipped with the ability to manage independence. Proper education today attends to the academic, social, moral and spiritual needs of a child in development. It is the aim of Oxfords International School to train a child along right lines fro the beginning. To teach him how to study and for correct habit of work and to inculcate the principles which are to regulate his daily conduct and guide his future life.

5.) To create an atmosphere of dress and action that sets the tone for the kind of behaviour that is acceptable in the society. Courtesy, cleanliness, helpfulness, honesty, being a good friend, resonsibility for one's actions, and love are all positive moral values we directly teach to the students.

6.) Through a carefully planned academic, athletic, social, and moral curriculum, Oxfords International School fosters in students, a growth process aimed at self-esteem and self-discipline, and with confidence and competence, our students are prepared to tackle new challenges


Our General Philosophy

Our General Philosophy

1.) We believe that the Oxfords International School makes an important contribution to the fundamental education status, moral and spiritual well-being of the Aba people. We believe that to continue to serve the community, we must be willing to modify, adapt and create opportunities that meet the ever-changing needs of students.


2.) We believe that it is our role to help students to learn to think independently, value logical and tested conclusions, develop problem-solving abilities, communicate well and function effectively with other people. We believe in the dignity and worth of the individual child and therefore provide educational opportunities regadless of age, economic or social background, or enrolment status.


3.) We believe that for continued growth, we must display the ability to be creative, strive for excellence and to exhibit leadership in the expansion of knowledge and skills through the achievements of staff and students.


4.) We hope to develop in our students the desire to continue their education throughout their lives. Oxfords' concern is its students and as such, this concern is reflected primarily through offering programmes of substantial quality with expectation that students will achieve a high level of competence and understanding in an atmosphere of positive engagement and mutual respect.


5.) At Oxfords, though standards are high, there's a unique emphasis placed upon a child's effort and personal achievement, hence, allowing everyone to be a winner. All Oxfords policies and schedules ae tailored to the needs of the children on individual basis.


6.) Excellence, Dedication, and Discipline live and breathe as the basis of education at Oxfords. These three words are incorporated into every aspect of Oxfords academic and non-academic programmes.
