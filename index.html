OXFORDS PROGRAMMES

ACADEMIC PROGRAMMES

At Oxfords, all subjects and learning experiences are geared towards the need of the learner and equipping him to tackle and succeed in the face of greater challenges in the future. Our staff are professionals in their areas and in addition to that, they undergo regular enrichment and refresher courses to get acquainted with current trends in the field of education and child training. The academic staff teach, guide and administer tests, make extensive evaluations and recommendations and provide individuals skills instruction. They confer regularly with the counsellor to suggest strategies and to monitor a child's progress, and maintain steady communication with parents. The new national policy on education and the use of modules for teaching and uniform continous assessment in Nigerian schools is the pivot around which Oxfords academic programmes revolve.

Oxfords International Nursery School

(Pre-Primary Education)

This is the educational programme packaged by Oxfords for children from the ages of about three(3), prior to their entering the primary school. Here, emphasis is laid on basic concepts of recognition and identification, beginners' language principles, fundamental numbers/counting, introductory writing strokes, etc.

Oxfords International Primary School

This is the package meant for children who must have attained the age of six(6). The course is for a six-year duration. The objectives include the preparation of a broad-based education with emphasis on the attainment of permanent and functional literacy and numeracy, and effective communication skills. Oxfords International Secondary School

In the Oxfords International Secondary School, education is given in two stages: the junior secondary school and senior secondary school. Each stage runs for a duration of three(3) years. The broad aims of these programmes are preparation for useful living within the society and for higher education. The junior secondary school is both prevocational and academic.

Extension Programmes

Oxfords International School in a bid to living up and maintaining its international status, offers special extension, preparatory and enrichment programmes to cater for the academic and professional needs of scholars. Our team of educationists and educators administer tutorials primarily geared towards enhancing performance in international standard examinations and facilitating admission into universities and colleges overseas. Candidates also enjoy the services of our overseas counsellors who guide and instruct them on study opportunities abroad and procedures and procedures for obtaining international scholarship

Oxfords special extension programmes include:

1.) SAT I/ SAT II: Scholastic Assessment / Aptitude Test

2.) PSAT / NMSQT: Preliminary Scholarship Aptitude Test/National Merit Scholarship Qualifying Test

3.) ACT: American College Test

4.) GMAT: Graduate Management Admission Test

5.) GRE: Graduate Record Examinatioon

6.) TOEFL: Test Of English as a Foreign Language

7.) TWE: Test of Written English

8.) TSE: Test of Spoken English

9.) IELTS: International English Language Testing System

10.) London / Cambridge Oxford GCE

11.) English Proficiency COurse for Secretaries and Business Executives

12.) Adult Education

SPORTS AND OTHER CO-CURRICULA PROGRAMMES

Students' involvement in athletics - inter scholarstic, intramoral, extramoral or recreational - is an integral and required part of Oxfords educational experiences.

With emphasis on commitment, skills improvement, teamwork, sportsmanship, and individual achievement, each child is encouraged to compete in internal and external contests involving his team, house or class. The fundamental purpose of Oxfords athletics is to engender in each student a life-long appreciation for physical activities, to become more experienced, skilled and confident in a variety of games, and above all, to make sports experience fun and rewarding. The annual inter-house sports competition is always a celebration.

Principal among these games are: basketball, volleyball, soccer, table tennis, sprints, jumps, throws and other minor and local games.

Emphasis is also laid on students' involvement and full participation in debate club, drama club, music and art club, community voluntary service, scripture union, young farmers' club, young orators' club and the scout movement of Nigeria.

COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMMES

Oxfords International School considers its commitment to providing high standard quality education at a very low tuition fee as part of its community development project. The Oxfords admission scholarship schemes are designed to give priority to our host community as a means of fostering cordial public relationship and providing for the community.

Periodic community cleanliness and voluntary service programmes are organized during which staff and students help out in the community maintaining roads, clearing bushes, providing drinking water and visiting the needy with material and money assitance.

It is our hope to extend these programmes further to include additional grounds.
