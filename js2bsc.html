OXFORDS E-LESSON 1 T3

CLASS : JS2

TOPIC : CRUDE AND PETROCHEMICALS

BREAKDOWN : MEANING OF CRUDE & PETROCHEMICALS, REFINING OF CRUDE OIL, USES

LEARNING OUTCOME : Explain crude oil & petrochemicals, describe the process of refining crude oil, and uses/importance of crude oil and petrochemicals.


CRUDE OIL

This is the dark brown or greenish flammable liquid of the unrefined petroleum found under the ground or sea. Crude oil consist of complex mixture of various hydrocarbons, small particles of sulphur and metals

PETROCHEMICALS

These are chemical compounds which are derived from refining petroleum, e.g ethanol, ethene, propanol e.t.c... These small molecules of organic compounds are used to make large molecules of organic compounds like plastics, synthetic rubber, detergents, insecticides etc...

REFINING OF CRUDE OIL

Crude oil is a complex mixture of hydrocarbons and to separate the constituents, fractional distillation method is applied. This is a process whereby petroleum is separated into diffferent fractions. Each fraction contains compounds that fall into a certain boiling point range. The fractions can be differentiated from each other by their different texture, color, odour, volatility, e.t.c. Examples of products of refined crude oil include;
petroleum gas, petrol, naphtha, kerosene, diesel oil, lubricating oil, bitumen.

USES OF PETROCHEMICALS

i.) Raw materials for the production of polymers.

ii.) Fertilizers

iii.) Pesticides

iv.) Food additives

v.) Detergents

vi.) Cosmetics

IMPORTANCE OF CRUDE OIL AND PETROCHEMICALS

They are:

i.) Sources of revenue.

ii.) Sources of energy.

iii.) Products and sources of industrial raw materials.

ASSIGNMENT

1.) Distinguish between crude oil and petrochemicals.

2.) Name two(2) by-products of refined crude oil.

3.) Mention five(5) uses of petrochemicals.

4.) Mention three(3) importances of crude oil to Nigeria.
